NATASHA KOSTEWICZ

DIGITAL ARTIST

Natasha is a Filipino-American illustrator and concept artist interested in writing, creating music, drawing, and anything creative. She currently lives in Orlando, Florida as a student at the University of Central Florida as a Digital Media major with a minor in Applied Linguistics.

Work and Goals

Touching upon themes of youth and religion, Natasha's illustrations are both vibrant and melancholic, drawing inspiration from the classics and her own real world experiences. In the future, she hopes to apply her knowledge of these themes to design concepts, both character and environmental, for fantasy games and series.
